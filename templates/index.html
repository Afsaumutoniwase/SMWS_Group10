Frequently Asked Questions

To schedule a waste collection, log into your account, navigate to the 'Schedule Collection' section, and select your preferred date and time. Make sure your bin is placed at the designated pickup spot.

Our system allows for the recycling of paper, plastic, glass, and metal. Ensure that these items are clean and dry before placing them in the recycling bin. Check our detailed recycling guide for more information.

If your waste was not collected as scheduled, please report it through the 'Report Issue' section of your account. Provide details about the missed collection, and our team will address it as soon as possible.

Our Smart Waste Management System helps reduce landfill waste, increases recycling rates, and lowers carbon emissions by optimizing collection routes and schedules. Your participation contributes significantly to a cleaner environment.

You can track your waste management activities through your account dashboard. It provides detailed reports on your scheduled collections, recycling contributions, and environmental impact.
